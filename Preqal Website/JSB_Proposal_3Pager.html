Our Proposal
Preqal proposes a comprehensive, integrated implementation of ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management) and ISO 45001:2018 (Occupational Health & Safety) for JSB Investments Inc. and its subsidiary operations. Over nine structured months, our certified consultants will design, document, train, and guide JSB's teams to full certification readiness — with a single, integrated management system built to serve the entire group.
The Opportunity
  • Establish competitive advantage in Guyana's growing oil & gas services supply chain through demonstrated QMS compliance
  • Satisfy Local Content Register requirements and position JSB for Tier 1 supplier designation
  • Reduce workplace incidents, liability exposure, and operational disruptions through structured OH&S controls
  • Build internal capacity that sustains certification beyond the initial project — a system JSB owns and operates

02 — Understanding of the Client
JSB Investments Inc. — Context & Strategic Fit
JSB Investments Inc. is a Guyanese conglomerate with a diversified portfolio spanning construction, real estate development, engineering services, logistics, and industrial supply. The group operates multiple subsidiaries and is positioned as an active participant in Guyana's rapidly expanding oil & gas economy. JSB is registered on the Local Content Register and maintains ambitions to grow its supplier designation within ExxonMobil's and other operators' supply chains.
Source: JSB Investments website, GY Local Content Secretariat public register, Stabroek Business reports (2024–2026)
Key Business Drivers
  • ▸Eligibility for Tier 1 and Tier 2 oil & gas supplier contracts requiring verified QMS
  • ▸Growing construction and engineering workforce creating OH&S liability exposure
  • ▸Investor and partner due diligence increasingly requiring ISO certifications
  • ▸Multi-subsidiary complexity requiring group-level governance and process harmonisation
Standards Being Implemented
ISO 9001:2015
Quality Management System — customer focus, risk-based thinking, process approach, continual improvement
ISO 45001:2018
OH&S Management System — hazard identification, worker participation, incident prevention, legal compliance
