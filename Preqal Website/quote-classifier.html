How It Works

Our Fair Classification Model

Preqal prices based on what your business actually needs — not a one-size-fits-all rate. We look at three things.

Staff Size

The number of people in your organisation forms the foundation of your tier. Larger teams have more complex management needs.

Number of Services

Each service your company provides requires its own documented management system, policies, and quality controls.

Processes per Service

Each service is broken into processes. More processes mean more documentation and audit scope — roughly 5 processes per service as a baseline.

Our Pricing Principles

  • 1 service is approximately 5 processes — we keep this simple for you.
  • Smaller companies should never pay more than larger companies for equivalent scope.
  • Pricing reflects company size first, with a complexity adjustment for scope.
  • All quotes are reviewed and confirmed by a Preqal specialist — this is an estimate only.
  • All engagements run for a 9-month programme — enough time to implement and achieve certification.
  • High process complexity upgrades your tier to ensure you receive the right level of support.
