② Parallax Rings

Concentric rings move at different scroll speeds — 0.3×, 0.6× relative to viewport scroll. Pure CSS transform.

Embedded Spline scene — a slow-rotating 3D sphere with amber-to-dark shading. Draggable by user. ~400KB gzipped.

④ Framer Motion

Headline, subtitle, and CTA buttons stagger in on load (already have Framer Motion). Glass card effect on content block.
