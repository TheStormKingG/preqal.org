Implementation notes

Video delivery

Strip audio via ffmpeg → serve as muted MP4 + WebM pair. <video muted autoplay loop playsinline>. No GIF — better quality, smaller file.

Ring animation

CSS @keyframes — scaleY collapses the ring as it rotates edge-on, making it look like a true 3D orbit. Inner ring 8s, middle 14s, outer 22s.

mix-blend-mode

multiply removes the black video background on the light neumorphic base — no masking or alpha channel needed.

Mobile fallback

Video lazy-loads below the fold on mobile. Static Image1.webp shown as poster frame. Rings simplify to 1 non-animated ring on reduced-motion.
