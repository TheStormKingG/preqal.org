Client Pipeline — Full Flow

Here's the complete system we're designing. Review the flow and let me know if anything needs to change before we go deeper.

1
Lead Capture
Quote Request Form (preqal.org/services or /book)
Client selects desired services (Steps 1–8, cumulative), provides business info. Form populates → quote_submissions table + REG-02 Qualified Leads Register. Auto-email sent to Stefan.
Company info Service steps selected Staff size / services count Contact details
2
Admin · Qualified Leads Register
"Prepare Quote" Button → Quote Creation Wizard
Admin sees lead in the Qualified Leads table. Clicks "Prepare Quote" → modal wizard opens.
Quote Wizard — 3 Steps
Step 1
Review Lead Info
Pre-filled from register. Edit if needed.
Step 2
Tier Selection
System suggests T1–T6. Human overrides.
Step 3
Review Quote PDF
Auto-generated. Click "Approve & Send".
→ Button: "Quote Sent" (disabled) → "Onboard" button enabled
3
Admin · After Quote Accepted
"Onboard" Button → Onboarding Wizard
Client verbally/email accepts quote. Admin clicks "Onboard" → wizard opens.
Onboarding Wizard — 2 Steps
Step 1
Review Agreement
Service Agreement auto-filled from quote. Reviewed as PDF.
Step 2
Approve & Send
Email with Service Agreement + unique Client Onboarding Form link.
→ Button: "Onboarding Form Sent" (disabled)
4
Client-Facing
Client Onboarding Form (preqal.org/client-onboarding?token=…)
Client opens their unique link. Company details pre-filled. They confirm / edit and provide legal details.
Company name + address + reg # Rep name + ID/Passport + contact Contract start date Kickoff meeting date
→ Saves to crm_clients · Admin notified · CRM auto-updated
